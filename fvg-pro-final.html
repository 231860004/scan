⚡ GODZILLA FORMULA — ALL MUST PASS
① BTC VOLUME GATE
Altcoins only scanned when BTC hourly volume > 1.3× its 24h average.
High BTC volume = market moving = altcoins follow.
Gate CLOSED → no signals fired, saving you from losing trades.
② ORDER IMBALANCE I = (Vbid − Vask) / (Vbid + Vask)
Averaged between order book depth (top 20 levels) + last 100 trades buy/sell flow.
I > 0 = buyers dominant → only LONG signals pass
I < 0 = sellers dominant → only SHORT signals pass
Minimum |I| = 0.05 (5% imbalance required)
③ GARCH VOLATILITY σ = √(λσ²t−1 + (1−λ)r²t)
EWMA (λ=0.94) forecasted volatility from last 30 candle returns.
σ > 2.5% per candle → TOO VOLATILE — signal blocked
Low σ → bonus confidence points added
High vol = wide unpredictable moves = fake signals
④ BTC DIRECTION ALIGNMENT
LONG altcoin signal → BTC must be trending UP in last 4 hours
SHORT altcoin signal → BTC must be trending DOWN in last 4 hours
Altcoins follow BTC — trading against BTC trend = low win rate
⑤ VOLATILITY-ADJUSTED LEVERAGE MaxLev = 1/(σ × L₀)
L₀ = 0.5 (base factor). High volatility → leverage automatically reduced.
Example: σ=1% → MaxLev=200, σ=5% → MaxLev=40, capped at user setting.
Protects you from overleveraging during volatile markets
